# Resources and Communities

Curate a few trusted places to learn and ask questions. Participate; do not just lurk.

## Learning platforms
- Khan Academy (math and physics fundamentals)
- MIT OpenCourseWare (university-level courses)
- Coursera/edX (structured sequences across disciplines)

## Maker and code
- r/arduino, r/raspberry_pi for hardware tinkering
- Stack Overflow for programming questions (read guidelines first)
- Hackaday and Adafruit guides for hands-on builds

## Science and systems
- LessWrong/Alignment Forum for rationality and AI safety debates
- NASA and ESA outreach sites for space science explainers
- PubMed and bioRxiv for primary biology research (start with reviews)

## Writing and rhetoric
- r/writing and local writing meetups
- Toastmasters for public speaking practice
- Write the Docs for technical communication

## Meetups and maker spaces
- Local maker spaces and hacklabs for tools and mentors
- Science cafes or astronomy clubs for observation nights
- Amateur radio clubs for electronics and communication practice
